A Name That Broke the Dark

A name is whispered—a spark in the dark,

A fire born where hope once sank.

A tethered voice, a guiding thread,

It pulls him back from where he bled.

The monster screams, it claws, it cries,

But ghosts dissolve in morning skies.

No voice remains, no second fate—

He’s whole at last, the hour’s late.

The ship soars on, beyond the tide,

No echoes left, no one inside.

Only one man, bound for grace,

And one small name, that lit his face.
