Task 2 : Gyroscopic Calibration

The Spacecraft is spinning in irregular intervals thus destroying the stability it. But you seem to have forgotten the calibration codes. You look around the spacecraft in a frantic search to find the codes.
You then see the current position of the spacecraft in terms of X-axis, Y-axis, Z-axis in the console as (4,5,9) if the blackhole is treated as the origin (0,0,0) and 3 sets of equations beside the console.

Maybe solving them will give you the codes for X, Y, Z axes?
